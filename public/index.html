1's and 0's, Knits and Purls

Coding is a simple concept - given certain inputs and a desired output, figure out the way to get there. It's beautiful in its simplicity to pull together an objective goal with a subjective way of achieving that goal.

Knitting gives me a way to tangibly touch my code and my projects. Knititng has two basic stitches - knitting and purling, which every work is comprised of - not unlike 0's and 1's in programming! I have been knitting for more than 10 years - check out my knitting page to see some of my favorite projects.

I have been programming also for about 10 years. I started out with Visual Basic in high school, moved onto Java in college, worked with MATLAB and a little C++ in grad school, and currently, work with Python/Django. Check out my coding page to see some highlighted projects as well as some projects I am currently working on.
